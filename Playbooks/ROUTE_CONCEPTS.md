## 1. Coverage Beaters (The "Rock, Paper, Scissors" of Passing) 👊✋✌️

| If the Defense Plays...            | Their Weakness 📉                         | The "Killer" Routes/Concepts 🚀           | Why it works / Where to throw                                                |
| :--------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Cover 0 (Zero Blitz)**           | No deep help; 1-on-1 everywhere.          | **Go/Fade, Slant, Zig, Screen**           | Throw deep immediately or hit a slant/screen hot.                            |
| **Cover 1 (Man Free)**             | 1 Safety deep; Man underneath.            | **Mesh, Drag, Slant, Double Moves**       | Crossing routes rub defenders off; double moves win vs man.                  |
| **Cover 2 (Zone)**                 | Deep sidelines (Honey hole); Middle deep. | **Smash (Corner), Fade (sideline), Post** | Throw the corner route over the flat defender, or the post between safeties. |
| **Cover 3 (Zone)**                 | Seams (Hashes); Flats.                    | **4 Verts (Seams), Curl-Flat, Flood**     | Throw the seams between the deep 3rd defenders; or hit the flat quickly.     |
| **Cover 4 (Quarters)**             | Underneath; Intermediate middle.          | **Digs, Curls, Stick, Draw Run**          | Safeties bail deep fast; tons of space for 10-yard stop routes.              |
| **Cover 6 (Quarter-Quarter-Half)** | Weak side flat; Deep middle.              | **Flood (field side), Post-Dig**          | Attack the Cover 2 side with Smash; attack the Cover 4 side with Digs.       |

## 2. Route Concepts by "Purpose" 🎯

### Man Beaters (Vs Tight Coverage)

_Requires WRs with good Release (REL) and Short Route Running (SRR)._

1.  **Zig / Whip:** The ultimate man-beater. Fakes in, breaks out. DBs get lost.
2.  **Drag / Shallow Cross:** fast run across the field. Speed wins.
3.  **Texas (RB Angle):** RB fakes flat, cuts sharply inside. LBs can't cover this.
4.  **Slant:** Quick inside leverage win.

### Zone Beaters (Vs Soft Coverage)

_Requires patience and finding the "window"._

1.  **Flood:** 3 routes to one sideline (Deep, Intermediate, Short). Overloads the zone.
2.  **Spot / Snag:** Triangle concept (Corner, Flat, Spot). Stretches defense horizontally.
3.  **Mesh:** Two shallow crossers sit down in holes vs zone (or keep running vs man).
4.  **Curl-Flat:** Pushes DBs back with the deep threat, hits the curl in the void.

### Blitz Beaters (Hot Reads)

_When they send the house, throw these NOW._

1.  **Slant:** Standard hot route. One step, throw.
2.  **Bubble Screen:** Get the ball to the edge before pressure arrives.
3.  **Pop Pass (TE):** Fake run, pop the ball to TE over the blitzing LBs.
4.  **Hitch:** 3-step drop, ball out.

## 3. Audibles Blueprint (What to set in your "Custom Audibles") 🗣️

Every playbook lets you set 4-5 audibles. Don't be random. Build a **toolbelt**.

| Audible Slot     | Recommended Call   | Purpose (When to call it)                                      |
| :--------------- | :----------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Run (Inside)** | Inside Zone / Dive | Call vs **Light Box** (High Safeties, Spread DL).              |
| **Pass (Quick)** | Stick / Slants     | Call vs **Blitz** or Soft Off-Coverage.                        |
| **Pass (Deep)**  | Verts / PA Shot    | Call vs **Press Man** (if WR is fast) or Safety creeping down. |
| **Cover Beater** | Flood / Smash      | Call vs **Cover 3 / Cover 2** Zone shells.                     |
| **Screen**       | Bubble / HB Screen | Call vs **Aggressive Pass Rush** to slow them down.            |

## 4. Reading the Defense 101 (Pre-Snap) 👀

**1. Check the Safeties:**

- **1 High Safety:** Likely Cover 1 (Man) or Cover 3 (Zone). -> _Think Seams, Crossers._
- **2 High Safeties:** Likely Cover 2, Cover 4, or Man-Under. -> _Think Run, Middle of Field._

**2. Check the Corners:**

- **Pressing (Close):** Likely Man or Cover 2 Hard Flat. -> _Fade/Go if fast; Drag/Mesh if slow._
- **Off (Back 5+ yds):** Likely Cover 3 or Cover 4. -> _Quick Hitch, Screen, Out route._

**3. Check the "Box" (LBs + DL):**

- **5 or fewer:** Run the ball! (Light box).
- **7 or more:** Pass! (Stacked box).
